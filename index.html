LockLatch and Custom Blinds Shutters & Awnings partnership

Custom Blinds Shutters & Awnings has announced a strategic partnership with LockLatch, uniting two South African innovators in home design and security. The collaboration introduces LockLatch’s patented locking system to the Custom Blinds audience, offering secure ventilation and design continuity for Garden Route homes.

LockLatch keeps windows and doors partially open while locked. Homes stay light and airy while privacy and security remain intact. Integrated with our range of window and outdoor solutions, the safety latch extends how we design for comfort and security every day. Learn more about safety bars and security screens.
